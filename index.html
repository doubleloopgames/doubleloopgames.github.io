Lead Game Designer

Double Loop Games is looking for a Lead Game Designer to design gameplay and content for a new, original mobile game.

Who we are

We are a well-funded mobile game start-up focused on making delightful, relaxing experiences for the biggest audience in games: people who don’t think of themselves as gamers.

We're building an environment that's transparent, respectful, and family-friendly. Our current team is in California and Oregon, but we're happy to work with people remotely anywhere in the Americas. Diverse candidates encouraged. Kindness expected.

Who do we want

Double Loop Games is looking for an experienced Lead Game Designer to help design and implement gameplay interactions and levels. The ideal candidate has a wealth of experience designing, testing, and tuning casual to midcore F2P gameplay systems, cinematics, levels and content. This designer will also be responsible for reviewing & giving feedback to bring a portion of the game to final polish level -- extremely high attention to detail is required. They should showcase a track record of high quality work and enthusiasm for their craft, and be experienced using Unity.

Responsibilities
  • Work with the Game Director to design F2P gameplay and metagame systems. Review and test them in game, owning the feedback process of bringing them to a final, shippable polish level
  • Use Unity and our custom tools to build levels and input/configure levels and large quantities of data for content unlocks & rollout. The Lead Game Designer will create first pass tuning, but an Economy Designer will provide final tuning
  • Work with a Narrative Designer and Artist to design tutorial & cinematic sequences that meld with gameplay. Review them in game, giving multiple rounds of feedback, and own the process of bringing them to final, shippable polish level
  • Design & iterate on content unlock & rollout
  • Integrate and finalize work in-engine
Qualifications
  • 4+ years industry experience
  • Portfolio showcasing F2P mobile games you have worked on
  • Ability to iterate quickly
  • Extreme attention to detail
  • Excellent communication and planning skills
  • Ability to design and configure large data sets with care
  • Experience with source control
  • Experience with Unity
  • Experience with using playtest feedback and live player data to re-tune data
  • Ability to communicate in English, both verbally and written
  • Conscientious and self-motivated
Huge plusses
  • Experience working in Github
  • Experience with pre-planning content & mechanic rollout and difficulty curves to tune against
  • Experience designing, reviewing, and finalizing tutorials/narrative/cinematics
  • Experience managing and mentoring other designers
  • Experience balancing portions of a F2P economy
  • A love of relaxing, delightful gameplay, and beautiful worlds filled with both outdoor spaces and intimate village life
  • A strong desire to step outside of your comfort zone and learn new things

Quality Assurance Lead

Double Loop Games is looking for a Quality Assurance Lead with experience working on F2P live operated mobile games, to create testing processes, perform in-house testing, oversee external QA testers, to ensure high quality releases.

Who we are

We are a well-funded mobile game start-up focused on making delightful, relaxing experiences for the biggest audience in games: people who don’t think of themselves as gamers.

We're building an environment that's transparent, respectful, and family-friendly. Our current team is in California and Oregon, but we're happy to work with people remotely anywhere in the Americas. Diverse candidates encouraged. Kindness expected.

Who do we want

Double Loop Games is looking for a QA Lead who has deep knowledge and experience in testing live-operated mobile games, ensuring bug-free live updates, player account integrity, and exceptional player-facing experience. The QA Lead will research submission requirements, create testing procedures and workflows according to best practices, and work closely with Production, Design, Engineering, Art and Data to develop test plans. They will also perform in-house testing and reporting, and help select and then manage external QA testers when we need to scale up test capacity for live launches.

You would be a perfect match for this position if you thrive in a heavily collaborative and iterative environment, you are enthusiastic about developing testing processes, managing bug databases, and ensuring high quality launches, and you are excited by the opportunity to make a visible impact on our games.

Responsibilities
  • Ensure the team understands and uses best practices for testing live-operated mobile games, including versioning, test staging, cloning live accounts to test with, and more
  • Research and maintain latest information on submission requirements to the App Store, Google Play, etc.
  • Develop functional test cases and test plans curated to specific phases of development, (including Production, limited Soft Launch, and Global Launch), and specific title features, and mock review feedback
  • Perform functional and compliance testing, overseeing the execution of test plans and enforce accurate bug reporting
  • Work closely with Production to set up submission and test accounts with 1st Party App Stores, and to test and submit builds
  • Triage bugs; review all issues entered by internal and external teams to ensure bug reports include required information, are properly assigned, and conform to Double Loop QA standards
  • Reviews all disputed (‘Not a Bug,’ ‘Will Not Fix,’ and ‘Duplicate’) issues with Production
  • Help to evaluate and choose external QA vendors
  • Manage QA resources and schedule for yourself and external test teams that will be scaled up and down through development phases
  • Help select, and then lead an external QA test team. Act as their main point of contact, communicating their priorities and requirements, monitoring and evaluating their bug quality, and ensuring that proper workflow is followed.
  • Review and assess compliance reports from App Stores
  • Work with engineering to automatically generate meaningful daily reports on title quality and stability
  • Create daily or weekly, as needed, reports summarizing title quality, stability, and issues for stakeholders
  • Communicate significant game breaking and qualitative issues to Producers and company leadership
  • Research, document, and maintain a knowledge base of first-party test tools, technical requirements, and best practices
  • Progressively refine test and general processes for continuous improvement in development and live operations
  • Coordinate the setup of hardware and test resources
  • Manage bug database onboarding, usage, and reporting quality for internal and external team
  • Understand and embrace the constraints of a game development cycle (you know when bug fixing, and optimization become more important than new features.)
Qualifications
  • Experience with bug tracking solution required (JIRA / DevTrack / Bugzilla / etc.); experience with JIRA strongly preferred
  • 2+ years in a Lead QA role on a mobile F2P game, live operated at scale
  • 4 years total video game QA experience
  • Able to identify defects in video games and report defects in a clear and concise manner
  • Able to compare implementation in-game against specifications and report variances in a clear and concise manner
  • Capacity to create and implement innovative testing procedures and materials
  • Experience coordinating with external contractors preferred
  • Excellent communication, planning, and organization skills
  • Extreme attention to detail
  • Ability to communicate in English, both verbally and written
  • Conscientious and self-motivated
Huge plusses
  • Experience with Unity
  • Experience with profiling tools (Unity's profiler, Instruments, Snapdragon profiler)
  • Experience with testing analytics
  • A love of relaxing, delightful gameplay, and beautiful worlds filled with both outdoor spaces and intimate village life
